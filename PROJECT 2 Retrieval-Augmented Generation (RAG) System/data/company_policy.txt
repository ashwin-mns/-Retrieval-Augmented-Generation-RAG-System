# Company Policy: Remote Work

## Introduction
This document outlines the remote work policy for AI Innovations Corp.

## Eligibility
All employees who have completed their 3-month probationary period are eligible for remote work, subject to manager approval.

## Core Hours
Regardless of location, all employees must be online and available during core hours: 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM EST.

## Equipment
The company provides a laptop and a $500 home office stipend for eligible employees.

## Security
Employees must use the company-approved VPN for all work-related activities.

## Contact Information
For questions regarding this policy, contact HR at hr@ai-innovations.com or visit the internal portal.
